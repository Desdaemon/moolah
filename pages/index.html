Budget Calculator

Moolah can help you budget for your month

Moolah works with the 50-30-20 rule of budgeting.

  • 50% - Needs or necessities of the month
  • 30% - Wants or leisure spending
  • 20% - Savings of different forms such as Investing, Savings accounts and 401Ks

Moolah also gives provides you information on how much to keep aside for state and federal taxes.
